TypographyTo blend the "Creative Director" and "Researcher" vibes, we need a high-contrast typography pairing.Headings (Creative Director): Space Grotesk or IBM Plex Sans. Geometric, modern, and structural.Body & Insights (Researcher): Merriweather or IBM Plex Serif. Highly readable, gives the immediate impression of an academic paper or journal.Code & Tech (Developer): Fira Code or JetBrains Mono. Used for the Playground API inputs/outputs.3. Page Layouts & UX PatternsGlobal Structural Element: The site features a subtle 1px grid background pattern. Sections are divided by hard horizontal and vertical lines, like an architectural blueprint.Home: Split-screen layout. Left side: The Option 2 elevator pitch in massive, bold typography. Right side: An interactive, simplified terminal or graphic that visualizes an AI input/output. CTA: "Explore the Playground" & "Contact NurAmirah."Portfolio: A horizontal timeline. Instead of a traditional resume, it looks like a project roadmap or a Gantt chart, tracking your journey, knowledge, and achievements.Insights: Styled exactly like an arXiv research paper or a clean PDF. Two-column text layout, serif fonts, abstract diagrams. Very academic.My Playground: A dashboard-style layout.Left Sidebar: List of the 7 live experiments.Center Console: The interactive demo (chat interface, generated output, etc.).Right Panel: "The Architecture" (brief explanation of the model) and the "Acquire / Log Interest" form.
